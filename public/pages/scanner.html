Scanner Tips
  • Make sure your physical barcode scanner is connected
  • Click "Focus Scanner" or click in the input box before scanning
  • Most scanners automatically press Enter after scanning
  • For quick transactions, scan an item first, then use the quick buttons
  • To add a new item with an existing barcode, scan it and follow the prompts
